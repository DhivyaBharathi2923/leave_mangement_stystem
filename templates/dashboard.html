Leave Mangement module

online student leave management Software made to ease the complete leave management process for your leave easy.Our Leave management module is designed to capture each leave policies such as medical leave, emergency leave ,permission,on-duty etc. Effortless Leave Requests, Instant Approvals, Real-time Tracking - Experience the Future of Leave Management.

Apply leave

Allow Student to apply for leave on their own.You can also track each student's attendance history.With the records, you can analyze attendance trends to improve the course. You'll also have access to the reasons behind absences, allowing you to better understand student attendance patterns and make necessary adjustments.

Cancel Leave

Sometimes, the reason for taking leave might not happen. Allow your Student also to cancel leave on their own, so that you do not need to call them or reach them for particular reasons.

Approve/Reject Leave Requests

Staff play a crucial role in the leave management process. They are responsible for reviewing and making decisions on leave requests submitted by students. When a leave request comes in, Staff have the authority to either approve or reject it based on various activities such as valide reason, attendence percentage is low, and exam date..
